T700-GE-701C Engine

The T700-GE-701C engine is derived from the T700-GE-700 engine (85% common parts) with aerothermodynamic improvements necessary to achieve an approximate 20% increase in maximum power. The engine maintains the same basic structure and configuration. Improvements were made primarily in the Compressor Rotor Assembly, the Hot Section Module, and the Power Turbine Module.
